ORIGAMI DESIGNS

Interesting facts about Camel:-
*Camel's ears are furry.
*Camels can move easily on the sand because of their special padded feets.
*When they see water, they drink as much as possible

Interesting facts about Chemeleons:-
*Chemeleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder.
*Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chemeleons.
*Most Chemeleons have a prehensile tail that they use wrap around tree branches.
